# 01 — Product Vision

## One-sentence definition
**Sanzu.ai is a digital copilot that helps families in Portugal handle post-loss bureaucracy with a clear plan, ready documents, and minimal disruption to professional life.**

## Expanded definition
Sanzu.ai turns a fragmented and opaque set of obligations into an **executable workflow** that:
- defines “what next” and “who is responsible”
- collects required information once
- generates documents correctly
- coordinates multiple people safely
- provides transparent progress and an auditable trail

## Product positioning
### Sanzu is
- Post-loss bureaucracy copilot (Phase 1)
- Workflow orchestration + document automation platform
- Shared transparency layer between agency and family

### Sanzu is not
- Funeral logistics platform (ceremony ops)
- Memorial/grief therapy product
- Law firm / legal advisory service
- Generic storage tool

## Value pillars
| Pillar | What it delivers | Product proof |
|---|---|---|
| Clarity | “What do I do next?” | next-best-step + checklist |
| Execution | docs produced quickly | template engine + validations |
| Coordination | fewer calls/chaos | roles + shared dashboard |
| Efficiency | less time off work | reduced cycle time & rework |
| Cognitive relief | less overwhelm | one-step-at-a-time UX |

## Product principles (non-negotiables)
1. **Clarity > breadth**
2. **Neutral, professional tone**
3. **One source of truth per case**
4. **Simple human roles** (Manager / Editor / Reader)
5. **Accuracy is a feature** (templates + process governance)
6. **Default to safety** (privacy, least privilege, auditability)

## North Star
**Completed cases/month with high satisfaction** (Family + Agency).
