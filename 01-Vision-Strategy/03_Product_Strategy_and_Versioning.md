## Versioning doctrine
- **V1.x:** deterministic foundation (correctness, clarity, documents)
- **V2.x:** agency-scale operations + recommendations (reduce chaos)
- **V3.x:** automation + assisted submissions (approval-based)
- **V4.x:** mission control (evidence graph + orchestration + deep integrations)
- **V5.x:** expansion (estate/benefits layer, cross-border scenarios)

## Strategic constraints (do not break)
- Keep human roles simple (Manager/Editor/Reader)
- Avoid legal-advice positioning
- Auditability for any state transition or external action
- Privacy-first evidence handling (sensitive docs restricted)
