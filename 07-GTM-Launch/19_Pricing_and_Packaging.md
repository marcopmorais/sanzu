# 19 — Pricing and Packaging

## Pricing principles
- Price the **administrative execution** value (time saved, fewer calls, fewer errors)
- Keep early pricing simple to maximize adoption
- Separate Sanzu pricing from ceremony pricing

## Packaging options
| Model | Description | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|
| Per-case | €X per process | simple | variable revenue |
| Volume tiers | lower €/case at volume | encourages adoption | needs forecasting |
| Subscription | flat/month | predictable | harder to start |
| Bundle add-on | agency sells “premium admin package” | easiest upsell | price anchored to bundles |

## Suggested pilot pricing (structure)
- Per-case fee with volume discounts after thresholds
- Optional “Pro/Agency Ops” tier in V2 (multi-case dashboard + analytics)
