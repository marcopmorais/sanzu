# 17 — Roadmap, Releases and OKRs

## OKRs (Year 1)
| Objective | Key Results |
|---|---|
| Prove agency adoption | 15 agencies onboarded; 60% active managers |
| Deliver family clarity | Family NPS ≥ 40; status calls reduced 30%+ (agency self-report) |
| Improve efficiency | median cycle time ↓ 20%; time-to-next-action ↓ |
| Reduce rework | rework rate <10%; missing-doc loops ↓ |
| Sustainable unit economics | gross margin ≥75%; support minutes/case below target |

## Release sequence
- **V1.0 Foundation:** case+RBAC+vault+checklist+core templates (pilot-ready)
- **V1.1 Correctness:** broaden step library + validations + governance workflows
- **V1.2 Transparency:** activity feed, reader UX, “what changed” clarity
- **V2.0 Agency scale:** multi-case ops + workload + analytics
- **V3.0 Automation:** notification rules, connector framework
- **V3.1 Assisted submissions:** approvals + receipts + status tracking
- **V4.0 Mission control:** evidence graph + orchestration + polling
