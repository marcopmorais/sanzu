## Governance cadence
- Step library: quarterly review + incident-driven hotfix
- Templates: monthly review + hotfix pipeline
- Security review: per release
- Metrics review: weekly during pilot

## Operating model
- Deterministic correctness first
- Automation only under policy constraints and approvals
- Auditability is always on
