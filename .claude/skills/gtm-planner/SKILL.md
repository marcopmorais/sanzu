# Go-To-Market Planner

Plan, coordinate, and execute product and feature launches.

---

## Launch Tier Framework

| Tier | Scope | Lead Time | Cross-functional | Example |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| **T1 - Major** | New product / pivot | 8-12 weeks | Full company | New product line launch |
| **T2 - Significant** | Major feature / expansion | 4-6 weeks | Marketing + Sales + CS | New pricing tier, major integration |
| **T3 - Standard** | Feature release | 2-3 weeks | Marketing + PM | New dashboard, workflow improvement |
| **T4 - Minor** | Small improvement | 1 week | PM + Changelog | Bug fix, UI tweak, minor enhancement |

## Messaging Framework

### Positioning Canvas (April Dunford method)
1. **Competitive alternatives:** What would customers use if we didn't exist?
2. **Unique attributes:** What do we have that alternatives lack?
3. **Value:** What capability do those attributes enable?
4. **Target customer:** Who cares most about that value?
5. **Market category:** What context makes the value obvious?

### Message Hierarchy
```
One-liner: [Product] helps [audience] [achieve outcome] by [key differentiator]

Elevator pitch (30 sec):
  - Problem: [Pain point for target audience]
  - Solution: [What we built]
  - Differentiation: [Why us vs alternatives]
  - Proof: [Social proof / metric]

Feature messaging:
  - Headline: Outcome-focused (not feature name)
  - Subhead: How it works in one sentence
  - 3 benefits: Each with proof point
```

## Launch Timeline Template

### T1 Major Launch (8-12 weeks)

**Week -12 to -8: Strategy**
- [ ] Define target audience and ICP
- [ ] Complete competitive analysis
- [ ] Draft positioning and messaging
- [ ] Set launch KPIs and success criteria

**Week -8 to -6: Content & Assets**
- [ ] Create landing page copy
- [ ] Write blog post / announcement
- [ ] Design product screenshots and demos
- [ ] Prepare sales enablement materials
- [ ] Record product demo video

**Week -6 to -4: Channel Prep**
- [ ] Set up email campaigns (announcement + drip)
- [ ] Prepare social media content calendar
- [ ] Brief PR / analyst relations
- [ ] Prepare paid media campaigns
- [ ] Create in-app announcements

**Week -4 to -2: Enablement**
- [ ] Sales team training and battle cards
- [ ] CS team training and FAQ
- [ ] Support documentation and help articles
- [ ] Beta customer testimonials collected

**Week -2 to -1: Final Prep**
- [ ] QA all landing pages and links
- [ ] Test email sequences
- [ ] Load campaigns in marketing automation
- [ ] Prepare war room and escalation paths
- [ ] Final go/no-go meeting

**Launch Day:**
- [ ] Deploy product changes
- [ ] Publish landing page
- [ ] Send email announcement
- [ ] Publish blog post
- [ ] Activate social media
- [ ] Activate paid campaigns
- [ ] Internal announcement
- [ ] Monitor dashboards

**Week +1 to +4: Post-launch**
- [ ] Daily metric monitoring
- [ ] Collect initial feedback
- [ ] Iterate on messaging based on data
- [ ] Week 1 retrospective
- [ ] Month 1 launch report with KPI review

## Launch KPIs by Channel

| Channel | Awareness Metric | Engagement Metric | Conversion Metric |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Email | Open rate | Click rate | Signup/upgrade rate |
| Blog | Page views | Time on page, scroll depth | CTA click rate |
| Social | Impressions, reach | Engagement rate | Link clicks |
| Paid | Impressions | CTR | CAC, conversion rate |
| Product | Feature impression | Feature try rate | Feature adoption (D7) |
| Sales | Pipeline influenced | Demo requests | Win rate change |

## Post-Launch Measurement

**Week 1:** Awareness & activation metrics
- Did target audience see the launch?
- Are they trying the feature?

**Month 1:** Adoption & engagement metrics
- What % of target users adopted?
- How often are they using it?

**Quarter 1:** Business impact metrics
- Did it move the target KPI?
- What's the ROI of the launch investment?
