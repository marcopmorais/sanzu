# User Research Plan - Sanzu Phase 1

**Objective:** Validate core assumptions about family/agency pain points before V1 development  
**Timeline:** 8 weeks  
**Budget:** €3,000-€5,000  
**Owner:** Product Manager

---

## RESEARCH QUESTIONS

### Primary (MUST answer)
1. **Family Pain:** What % of time/energy is consumed by post-loss admin? (quantify)
2. **Agency Pain:** What causes the most rework and coordination overhead?
3. **Digital Adoption:** Will families use a digital tool during grief? (barriers)
4. **Value Proposition:** What would reduce stress most? (priority features)
5. **Pricing:** What is willingness-to-pay from agencies?

### Secondary
6. Current cycle time distribution (baseline for metrics)
7. Mobile vs desktop usage patterns
8. Trust dynamics (agency relationship)
9. Document management pain points
10. Language/literacy barriers

---

## RESEARCH METHODS

### Method 1: Family Depth Interviews (n=15-20)

**Who:**
- Recently bereaved in Portugal (3-12 months ago)
- Used a funeral agency
- Mix of: urban/rural, simple/complex cases, age 25-65

**Recruitment:**
- Partner with 3-5 pilot agencies (they ask recent clients)
- Incentive: €50 gift card per 60-min interview
- Target: 15 interviews minimum

**Interview Guide:**

**Opening (5 min)**
- Thank you for sharing this difficult experience
- Our goal: understand how to reduce bureaucratic burden
- Your answers will shape a tool to help future families

**Timeline Reconstruction (15 min)**
- Walk me through the first week after the loss. What did you need to do?
- What administrative tasks took the most time?
- Which tasks felt most confusing or stressful? Why?
- Did you need to take time off work? How much?

**Pain Point Deep-Dive (20 min)**
- What documents were hardest to obtain or fill out?
- How many times did you contact the agency for status updates?
- What information did you wish you had upfront?
- Were there any mistakes or documents you had to redo?
- Scale 1-10: How overwhelmed did you feel? What caused that?

**Solution Validation (15 min)**
- If there was a digital checklist showing next steps, would you use it?
- What would make you hesitant to use a digital tool during grief?
- Would you trust a tool provided by your funeral agency?
- Desktop vs mobile: which would you prefer?

**Wrap-up (5 min)**
- What's the #1 thing that would have helped most?
- Anything else we should know?

**Deliverables:**
- Interview transcripts
- Pain point frequency matrix
- Quantified metrics (hours spent, tasks abandoned, stress rating)

---

### Method 2: Agency Shadowing Study (n=3-5 agencies, 5 cases each)

**Who:**
- 3-5 pilot candidate agencies
- Observe 5 active cases per agency (mix of complexity)

**What to Observe:**
- Time allocation (calls, emails, doc review, rework)
- Communication patterns (who initiates, frequency)
- Document handling (how many iterations, errors)
- Workflow tools (current software, spreadsheets, paper)

**Data Collection:**
- Time-tracking log (15-min increments)
- Shadow 2-3 full days per agency
- Interview agency staff (Manager, admin) - 30 min each

**Key Metrics:**
- % of time on "coordination overhead" (status calls, doc requests)
- Average # of status calls per case
- Document resubmission rate
- Cycle time from case creation to closure

**Deliverables:**
- Time allocation breakdown by agency
- Common pain points (coded from observations)
- Baseline metrics for V1 comparison

---

### Method 3: Prototype Usability Testing (n=10 families)

**When:** Week 7-8 (after Figma prototype ready)

**Prototype Scope:**
- Invite acceptance flow
- Intake questionnaire (5 questions)
- Dashboard with next step
- Document upload
- Step completion

**Test Protocol:**
- Moderated remote sessions (60 min)
- Think-aloud method
- Incentive: €40 gift card

**Tasks:**
1. Accept invite and create account
2. Answer intake questions
3. Find and click "next step"
4. Upload a sample document
5. Mark a step as complete

**Success Criteria:**
- >70% complete all tasks without help
- <5 min to first action (after login)
- No emotional distress signals (pause, offer support)

**Deliverables:**
- Task completion rates
- Time-on-task metrics
- Usability issue severity matrix (critical/major/minor)

---

### Method 4: Pricing Survey (n=50+ agencies)

**When:** Week 9-10

**Method:** Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Meter

**Survey Flow:**
1. Show product demo (2-min video)
2. Ask 4 pricing questions:
   - At what price would this be too expensive to consider?
   - At what price would it start to seem expensive but still worth it?
   - At what price is it a great value?
   - At what price would it seem too cheap to trust?
3. Follow-up: Which pricing model do you prefer?
   - Per-case fee
   - Monthly subscription
   - Tiered (volume discounts)

**Recruitment:**
- Email list of 200+ agencies (industry association?)
- LinkedIn outreach to agency owners
- Target: 50+ responses

**Analysis:**
- Plot price sensitivity curves
- Identify Optimal Price Point (OPP)
- Segment by agency size (small/medium/large)

**Deliverables:**
- Pricing recommendation (€X-€Y range)
- Preferred pricing model
- Segmentation insights

---

### Method 5: Diary Study (n=10 families) [OPTIONAL - if time/budget allows]

**Duration:** 2 weeks

**Who:**
- Families currently in active post-loss admin (0-3 months)
- Recruited via agencies

**Protocol:**
- Daily 5-min check-in (via WhatsApp or SMS)
- Log: tasks completed, time spent, stress level (1-10), blockers
- Weekly 15-min interview

**Goal:**
- Capture real-time experience (vs retrospective bias in interviews)
- Understand task sequencing and blockers

**Incentive:** €100 per participant
